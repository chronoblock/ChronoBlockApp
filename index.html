Frequently Asked Questions

How is ChronoBlock different from other productivity apps?
Most apps focus on managing tasks in isolation. ChronoBlock structures your entire day by allocating your total waking hours into purposeful time blocks. Instead of endless task lists, you get a complete framework for intentional living.
Do I need to create an account or provide personal information?
No! ChronoBlock works immediately without any registration. Your data is stored locally in your browser, so we never collect, store, or have access to your personal schedule information.
What happens if I clear my browser data?
Your time blocks and tasks are stored in local browser storage. If you clear your browser data, you'll lose this information. However, ChronoBlock includes export/import functionality so you can back up your schedule data anytime.
Can I use ChronoBlock on multiple devices?
Since data is stored locally, each device maintains its own schedule. You can export your data from one device and import it to another, or use this feature to maintain different schedules for different contexts (work vs. personal).
Is ChronoBlock really free? What's the catch?
ChronoBlock is 100% free and open source. There are no premium features, no subscriptions, no ads. We believe effective time management should be accessible to everyone. The code is publicly available for full transparency.
How do I optimize my daily schedule using ChronoBlock?
Start by creating basic blocks for major activities. After using your schedule for a week, notice patterns‚Äîwhen do you have the most energy? Which blocks feel too long or too short? ChronoBlock makes it easy to edit and refine your time blocks until you find your optimal daily structure.
